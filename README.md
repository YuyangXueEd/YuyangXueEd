## 👋 About

I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the [University of Edinburgh](https://www.ed.ac.uk), working in the [VIOS group](https://vios.science) and as part of the [CHAI-UK](https://github.com/CHAI-UK) consortium. My research sits at the intersection of **AI agents**, **causality**, and **medical imaging** — I publish papers and ship tools, both matter to me. On the side I make small 2D games with [Guesswhat-Studio](https://github.com/Guesswhat-Studio) and write at [rasin.cyou](https://rasin.cyou).

## 🔭 Now

- 🤖 Building **agent systems for causal reasoning & education** ([EducAgent](https://github.com/CHAI-UK/EducAgent), [CAUST](https://github.com/vios-s/CAUST)) — recent submissions to top ML venues
- 📰 Maintaining **[Linnet](https://github.com/YuyangXueEd/Linnet)** — a personal AI morning briefing from arXiv / HN / GitHub
- 🧬 Working on **machine unlearning for medical imaging** — see featured work below
